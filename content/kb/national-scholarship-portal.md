Use the National Scholarship Portal to search scholarship schemes, register, submit an application, and track status updates.

Learners should keep documents such as identity proof, bank details, income certificate, and education records ready before applying.

The portal helps students check whether a scheme is open, read eligibility rules, and follow the official application workflow.
